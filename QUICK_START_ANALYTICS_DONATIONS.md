## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Google Analytics
1. **Set up goals** - Track specific actions (downloads, contact form)
2. **Create custom reports** - See exactly what you want
3. **Set up alerts** - Get notified of traffic spikes
4. **Link to Search Console** - See search performance

### For Newsletter
1. **Offer incentive** - "Get exclusive updates and new music first!"
2. **Keep it simple** - Just ask for email (not phone, address, etc.)
3. **Send regularly** - Monthly updates about new music
4. **Segment your list** - Separate fans, recruiters, collaborators

### For Donations
1. **Be transparent** - Explain how donations help
2. **Show impact** - "Your support helps me create more worship music"
3. **Thank donors** - Send thank you email automatically
4. **Multiple options** - Mobile Money, PayPal, Bank
5. **Suggested amounts** - UGX 5,000 / 10,000 / 20,000 / Custom
